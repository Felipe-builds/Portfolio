About

Who I am

I'm a games developer focused on the mechanics layer - movement systems, physics fidelity, and the feel of a game before anything else. Currently studying Games Development (Level 3) at Leeds City College.

I've shipped projects solo and through game jams: a full FPS built to replicate Quake Live's movement, a technically precise CS:S movement controller in Godot 4, and a modular weapon system. I care about making things that are actually good to play.

Outside of dev, I fence at Harrogate Fencing Club. I also shoot photos, and care a lot about fashion and how put together I look. I'm a native speaker of Portuguese and English, and speak some French.
